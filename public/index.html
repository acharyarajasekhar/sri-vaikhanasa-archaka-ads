Archaka Ads

Archaka Ads is a FREE Android App designed to help Archakas to find the vacancies in temples. It also lets anyone post such open positions to help the Archaka community.

Key Features

  • Archakas can get to know about the temples with vacancy.
  • Temple management or any concerned user can post their Ads.
  • Each Ad consists of details like contact, address with location on google maps, and facilities provided to Archaka such as accommodation, rice, LPG gas cylinder, etc...
  • Instant Notifications for new/updated Ads.
  • Report any abusive/inappropriate Ads with/without immediate hide from public view.
